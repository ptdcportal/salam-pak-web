My old passport has expired but my visa for Pakistan is still valid. Do I need to apply for new visa with the passport?

No. If your visa is still valid you can travel to Pakistan with your two passports (old and new), as long as the visa is valid, not damaged, and is the appropriate type of visa required for your principal purpose of travel.

Who can apply through Pakistan Online Visa System?

Nationals of the 191 Countries who are residing in the same countries can apply for the Pakistani Visa through Pakistan Online Visa System. Applicants will be called for an interview if required at the Pakistan Missions Abroad in the same country.

How to apply for Pakistani Visa through Pakistan Online Visa System?

Applicant can apply Pakistani Visa through Pakistan Online Visa System via https://visa.nadra.gov.pk/ – On the website, the applicant can register themselves to make applications under their desired category. For more details, please go through the detailed application guide available at https://visa.nadra.gov.pk/downloads/application_guide.pdf

Who is eligible to obtain a visa on arrival for Pakistan?

Citizens of 50 countries are eligible to obtain a visa on arrival for Pakistan for purposes of tourism, valid for a stay of up to 30 days in the country.

How to apply for Visa on Arrival for visiting Pakistan?

You can apply for two types of visas online:

• First Time (New): You can apply for a new visa if you do not possess a valid Pakistan visa.

• Extension: You can apply for a visa extension if you possess a valid Pakistan visa and you are residing in Pakistan.

Required Documents:

  • I. Photograph
  • II. Passport
  • III. Hotel booking Details

Duration and Validity:

  • • Single Entry for short duration less than 3 months (First Time entry).
  • • Duration up to and greater than 6 months (Extension).
  • • Citizens from these countries may be eligible for a Double Entry Tourist Visa.
  • • A Tourist Visa can be extended for up to 6 Months or more, subject to approval.

Processing Time:

  • • Entry (Up to 3 Months): 7-10 days (Working Days)
  • • Extension (Up to 6 Months): 7-10 days (Working Days)
  • • Extension (Greater than 6 Months): 4 Weeks (Working days)

Where can I exchange currency upon arrival/departure?

Bringing in of foreign currencies is permitted without any limit. A passenger can bring any amount of any foreign currency to Pakistan. All major airports have several exchange bureaus in all terminals, so you can get the right currency for your destination. Other than that, there are a lot of money exchange shops such as Western Union, in every small and big city.

Do I have to get a new sim for my phone while I am in Pakistan?

If you do not insert SIM of a Pakistan Mobile Company in your mobile set and continue using foreign SIM with roaming, then there is no issue of duty and taxes or registration of mobile set. However, if you insert SIM of a Pakistan Mobile Company, you will immediately get an SMS from the Pakistan Telecommunication Authority to get your phone registered within 60 days. The process of registration and payment of duty taxes can be completed online on this link: https://dirbs.pta.gov.pk/

Which season is best to visit Pakistan?

It depends on what the tourist wishes to see. Unlike most countries, Pakistan gives an experience in all 4 seasons throughout the year. In summer and spring, the best places are hilly regions or places with greenery. The following places are a must-see during summer as it shows the beautiful nature:

  • Hazara
  • Chitral
  • Skardu
  • Kalash
  • Naltar Valley
  • Abbotabad and Harnoi
  • Murree and many more.

Generally, winter is more preferred as compared to summers in Pakistan. Following places can be visited for winter sports activities such as skiing.

  • Hunza Valley
  • Swat
  • Gorakh hills
  • Deosai national park
  • Gilgit-Baltistan
  • Malam Jabba
  • Ziarat, Quetta and many more.

Can foreigners drive in Pakistan?

An International Driving Permit (IDP) is an identity document that allows the holder to drive a private motor vehicle in any country that recognizes IDPs. To be valid, the IDP must be accompanied by a valid driving license. http://ictadministration.gov.pk/services/international-driving-permit/
